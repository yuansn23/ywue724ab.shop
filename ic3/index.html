Our Key Functions

IC3 serves as America's official cybercrime reporting gateway, operated by the FBI and NW3C since 2000

SECURE

📧Accept Public Reports

Individuals, businesses, and financial institutions can report cyber fraud, hacking attacks, ransomware, phishing, and other internet crimes through our official website.

VERIFIED

📊Data Analysis

We analyze massive amounts of complaint data to identify trends, patterns, and techniques, providing valuable intelligence to FBI and other law enforcement agencies.

ACTIVE

How It Works

Simple steps to report cybercrime and help protect others

SECURE
1

Submit Report

Fill out our official complaint form at ic3.gov with details about the incident, including dates, methods, amounts, and any evidence like IPs, emails, or websites.

PROTECTED
2

Analysis & Storage

IC3 receives and analyzes your report through big data systems. Major cases are forwarded to FBI or other enforcement agencies for investigation.

TRACKED
3

Follow-up

You receive an IC3 Complaint Number for tracking purposes. Your report contributes to national cybercrime intelligence and prevention efforts.

Reportable Crime Types

We handle all internet-related criminal activities

💰Investment Fraud

Investment scams, cryptocurrency fraud, and virtual currency schemes targeting victims' financial assets.

🛒Online Shopping Scams

Fraudulent e-commerce websites, fake products, and payment fraud in online transactions.

🎣Phishing Attacks

Email phishing, fake websites designed to steal personal information, and identity theft schemes.

💳Identity Theft

Credit card fraud, stolen personal information, and unauthorized use of financial accounts.

📧Business Email Compromise

BEC attacks targeting businesses, wire fraud, and corporate email account takeovers.

🔒Ransomware & Hacking

Ransomware attacks, data breaches, unauthorized system access, and malware infections.
